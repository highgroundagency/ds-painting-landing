Commercial Painting

Large-scale projects. On schedule. On budget.

HOA repaints, multi-unit complexes, retail facades, office buildings, and industrial sites across Santa Cruz County. We coordinate around tenants, business hours, and parking restrictions — so the work happens without disrupting the people who actually use the building.

What's included

Built for scale.

Large-Scale Buildings

Multi-story exteriors, parking structures, retail centers, and industrial facilities. Our crew is sized to the project and we bring our own equipment — lifts, scaffolding, sprayers, and protection materials.

HOA & Property Management

We work with HOAs and property management companies on recurring maintenance contracts and full-complex repaints. Detailed scopes, color-matching to existing palettes, and clean tenant communication throughout.

Multi-Phase Scheduling

Big buildings rarely get painted all at once. We sequence the work so units, tenants, and businesses can keep operating — staging by building, by elevation, or by floor depending on the constraints.

Zero-Disruption Execution

No trash, no debris, no overspray on cars, no late-night noise. Tenants stay productive. Customers stay happy. The job gets done — quietly and on time.
