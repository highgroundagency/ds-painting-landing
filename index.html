How We Work — 07

Our proven 6-phase painting process.

This is why our paint lasts 10+ years. Every phase matters. Nothing is ever skipped.

01

Power Washing & Surface Cleaning

Every job starts with a thorough pressure wash to remove dirt, mold, and loose paint — creating a clean, bondable surface.

02

Detailed Preparation

Caulking, sanding, and priming every surface. This is where most contractors cut corners. We never do.

03

Full Protection & Masking

Complete protection of your property — windows, trim, plants, and walkways — before a single drop of paint is applied.

04

Professional Application

Spray, back-roll, and brush techniques applied at the correct mil thickness for maximum adhesion and longevity.

05

Final Touch-Ups & Quality Control

A thorough inspection and final touch-ups before we consider the job complete.

06

Client Walkthrough & Approval

You walk the job with us. We don't leave until you're 100% satisfied. No exceptions.

Common Questions — 11

FAQ

Because cheap paint jobs cost more in the long run. We've seen homeowners pay twice — once for the cheap job, again to fix it 18 months later when it peels. We charge what it costs to do it right the first time, with proper prep and premium coatings. That's it.
Most painters skip prep to save time. We don't. Every surface gets pressure-washed, sanded, caulked, and primed before any paint goes on. The prep is what makes the paint last 10+ years instead of peeling in two. It's not glamorous work, but it's what separates a professional job from a weekend warrior.
Exclusively premium Sherwin-Williams coatings. We spec the correct product for every surface type — the primer matters as much as the topcoat. We don't use big-box store paint. The coatings we apply are commercial-grade products designed for durability and UV resistance on the Central Coast.
If our application fails — peeling, cracking, or adhesion issues caused by our work — we come back and fix it at no cost. Period. We also honor all manufacturer warranties on the coatings we apply. We've been doing this for 35+ years and we stand behind every job.
A standard residential exterior takes 5–10 business days depending on size and condition. Interiors are typically 3–5 days. Commercial projects vary by scope. We'll give you a clear timeline during the estimate and we commit to it — our on-time completion promise isn't just marketing.
